Everything You Need to Run Your Practice

Built from the ground up for equine practitioners. No workarounds, no compromises.

Dashboard

Today's schedule, key metrics, overdue items, and a Needs Attention section so nothing slips through.

Clients & Horses

Owner profiles, horse records, discipline tags, activity logs, and searchable dropdowns. Every detail in one place.

Appointments

Book visits with duration slots, track status from upcoming to completed, and choose between yard or client-address callouts.

Smart Travel Time

Google Maps built in. Automatically calculates travel time between appointments and shows "leave by" times on your schedule.

Treatment Notes

SOAP-style clinical records covering subjective, assessment, palpation, treatment, and recommendations. All timestamped and linked to the horse.

Vet Reports

Generate professional clinical reports for referring vets with your practice branding. Print or share in one tap.

Body Chart

Mark findings on an anatomical horse diagram with tappable regions. Visual clinical records that speak louder than words.

Photos & Videos

Capture photos mid-treatment, upload videos and vet documents. Everything filed under the right horse with gallery filtering and sharing.

Invoicing

Branded invoices with your logo, a built-in service catalog, and editable line items. Email to clients in one tap.

Payments

Record full or partial payments, track outstanding balances per client, and mark invoices as paid. No more chasing spreadsheets.

Works Worldwide

Auto-detects your region and currency. Supports GBP, USD, EUR, AUD, NZD, and CAD with local bank detail formats on every invoice.

Mobile First

Built for life at the yard. Full experience on your phone, tablet, or desktop. Same app, every device.
